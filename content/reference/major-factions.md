- [[factions/aetheris-isles/council-of-three|Council of Three]]: Governs the Isles; balances island interests.
- [[factions/aetheris-isles/ascendant-guild|Ascendant Guild]]: Oversees sky farms and floatstone construction.
- [[factions/aetheris-isles/ascendant-wardens|Ascendant Wardens]]: Guard the [[locations/aetheris-isles/floatstone-towers|Floatstone Towers]].
- [[factions/aetheris-isles/sky-guard-corps|Sky Guard Corps]]: Patrols air lanes and escorts ships.
- [[factions/iyresa/council-of-drifts|Council of Drifts]]: Cascadea leadership for the five drifts.
- [[factions/iyresa/the-gilded-compass|The Gilded Compass]]: Merchant power steering high-value trade.
- [[factions/iyresa/black-basin-syndicate|Black Basin Syndicate]]: Controls Stonefall underbelly and smuggling.
