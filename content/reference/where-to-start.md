## Start with a Region
- Begin at [[regions/iyresa/index|Coast of Iyresa]] for trade hubs, cities, and faction drama.
- Explore [[regions/aetheris-isles/index|Aetheris Isles]] if you want sky travel, floatstone tech, and airship hooks.
- Check [[regions/sylphorne/index|Sylphorne]] for elven borders, forests, and quieter intrigue.

## If you’re a Caster
- Read [[world/systems/floatstone|Floatstone]] and [[world/systems/ascension-zone|Ascension Zone]] for how magic interacts with tech.
- Note regional faiths: [[religion/the-three-pillars|The Three Pillars]] (Isles) and [[religion/the-hollow-flame|The Hollow Flame]] (Iyresa).
- City-specific magic flavor: [[locations/iyresa/cascadea|Cascadea]] and [[locations/aetheris-isles/stormhold|Stormhold]].

## If you’re a Crafter
- Materials and trade: [[locations/aetheris-isles/black-tides|Black Tides]] shipping lanes, [[locations/iyresa/port-phyrella|Port Phyrella]] markets.
- Factions to contact: [[factions/aetheris-isles/ascendant-guild|Ascendant Guild]], [[factions/iyresa/the-gilded-compass|The Gilded Compass]].
- Tech hubs: [[locations/aetheris-isles/floatstone-towers|Floatstone Towers]], [[locations/aetheris-isles/westreach-port|Westreach Port]].

## If you want Quests
- City intrigue: [[factions/iyresa/black-basin-syndicate|Black Basin Syndicate]] vs [[factions/iyresa/council-of-drifts|Council of Drifts]].
- Dungeon/ruin dives: [[locations/aetheris-isles/underground-labyrinth|Underground Labyrinth]], [[locations/aetheris-isles/falling-star-tower|Falling Star Tower]], [[locations/iyresa/onyx-caves|Onyx Caves]].
- Travel challenges: [[locations/aetheris-isles/black-tides|Black Tides]] crossings or elevator politics in [[locations/iyresa/stonefall|Stonefall]].
