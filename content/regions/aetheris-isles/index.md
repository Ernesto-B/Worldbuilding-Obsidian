## At a Glance
- Floating archipelago sustained by floatstone towers and the Ascension Zone.
- Airship travel dominates; the Black Tides make sea routes perilous.
- Council of Three balances guilds, wardens, and temple orders.

## What Adventurers Notice First
Persistent winds, cliffside docks, and floatstone customs checks greet arrivals.

## Travel & Hazards
Airship lanes between main islands; ferries risk the Black Tides; cliff lifts link docks to sky farms.

## Current Tensions
Sabotage rumors around floatstone towers, sky piracy, and outside powers probing floatstone secrets.

## Adventure Hooks
- Recover relics from [[locations/aetheris-isles/falling-star-tower|Falling Star Tower]] before rivals do.
- Escort a floatstone convoy between [[locations/aetheris-isles/kaelyth-island|Kaelyth]] and [[locations/aetheris-isles/vorynth-island|Vorynth]] past sky raiders.
- Trace sabotage attempts on [[locations/aetheris-isles/floatstone-towers|Floatstone Towers]].
