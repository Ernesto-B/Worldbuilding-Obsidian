## At a Glance
- Elven homeland tucked past dense forests southeast of Iyresa.
- Travel relies on guided woodland paths and guarded passes.
- Relations hinge on old pacts and vigilant wardens at the borders.

## What Adventurers Notice First
Whispering canopy, watchful scouts, and humming waystones meet newcomers.
