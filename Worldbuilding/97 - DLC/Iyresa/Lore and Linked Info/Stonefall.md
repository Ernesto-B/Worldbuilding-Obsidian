# Description
- Stonefall is the lower city of [[Coast of Iyresa|Iyresa]] - a city that is buried within the depths of a crater. 
- The city is split into 5 [[Hollow|Hollows]], each signifying a different economic class, with the wealthiest of the city (lower middle class) occupying the first [[Hollow]], and the poorest occupying the fifth [[Hollow]].
