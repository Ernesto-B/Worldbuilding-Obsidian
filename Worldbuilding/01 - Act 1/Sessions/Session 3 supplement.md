## Crew Archetypes
### A. The Loyal Old Salt
**Personality**
- Served under Vincent for years
- Protective, suspicious of outsiders

**What They Say**
- Defends the captain instinctively
- Mentions habits and routines
- Notices “small changes” but rationalizes them

**Good Clues**
- “Cap always tied that knot himself.”
- “He hasn’t been below deck much.”

**Bad Questions Trigger**
- Accusations → shuts down
- Insults → morale hit

## B. The Nervous New Hand
**Personality**
- Green, jumpy, sleep-deprived
- Afraid of being blamed

**What They Say**
- Heard noises
- Saw shapes at night
- Afraid to name names

**Good Clues**
- Scream timing
- Shadowy figures
- Inconsistent sightings

**Pressure Response**
- Cracks under gentle reassurance
- Lies under intimidation
