## Public Face
- Calm, composed, pragmatic
- Speaks in terms of balance, necessity, and survival
- Publicly blames pirates for the storm
- Positions himself as a stabilizing force

## Private Truth
- Knows the storm cannot be “cleanly” ended
- Fears exposure more than failure
- Believes secrecy is the only thing holding the Isles together

## Why He Does Not Trust Zorra
- She asks the wrong questions
- She challenges moral shortcuts
- She would expose the truth if she knew it
- He sees her as:
    - Naive
    - Dangerous to stability
    - A liability he cannot fully control

## His View of Zorra
- Loves her
- Underestimates her resilience
- Believes she will break under real consequences
- Keeps her politically close, operationally distant
